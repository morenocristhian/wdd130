Overview

Purpose

Our purpose as a company is to train people capable of participating in this competitive sport and to become professionals to form teams and participate in tournaments.

Audience

The primary audience includes individuals or groups looking for an exciting, physically challenging, and memorable rafting. Adventure Seekers Question: Who is the ideal customer seeking a rafting adventure? Answer: The ideal customer is an adventure seeker who enjoys adrenaline-pumping experiences and is looking for an exciting outdoor activity that combines teamwork and nature. Group Outings Question: What type of audience might be interested in group rafting experiences? Answer: The company caters to various audiences, including corporate groups, team-building events, or social gatherings, who seek unique and exhilarating group activities to strengthen bonds and create lasting memories. Family-Friendly Adventures Question: Is rafting suitable for families, and who would be the target audience? Answer: Yes, the company can target families seeking safe and enjoyable adventures. The audience includes parents looking for outdoor activities to share with their children, providing a memorable experience while enjoying the beauty of nature.
